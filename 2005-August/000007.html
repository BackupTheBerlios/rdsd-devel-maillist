Hello,
I am pleased to announce that the RDS related kernel stuff was accepted into 
the Video4Linux CVS. This is in detail

* the saa6588 module
* the rds.h header file
* a change to bttv-driver.c that enables reading from /dev/radio and making 
use of saa6588.ko

So, the time is near where RDS data can be read from /dev/radio using a 
standard kernel. Until then, you must check out a recent copy of Video4Linux 
CVS from linuxtv.org.

Have fun,
