Am Mittwoch, 11. Mai 2005 20:52 schrieb stanley kao:
> Hi, is there a list of supported radio tuner cards for
> this library?  

No, not yet. It should work with any supported radio card that has a SAA6588 
RDS decoder. All RDS cards I'm aware of use this chip. 

> Also I know that there are two 
> different standards RDS for Europe, and RBDS for US.
> Does this library only work for the RDS standard?

No, it'll support both. The differences are small. As I live in Europe, I can 
do most of the tests with RDS signals, but I keep RBDS in mind. The saa6588 
kernel module I'm writing at the moment has a module parameter that 
enables/disables RBDS mode.
