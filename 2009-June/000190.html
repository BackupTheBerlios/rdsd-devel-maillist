I'm pleased to announce that Edouard Lafargue joined the project. He does
interesting things with USB-connected radio devices, streaming RDS data
in ogg streams via icecast, and so on. He describes it in detail on his
homepage:

https://www.lafargue.name/article2773.html

I gave him write permission to the rdsd SVN repository, so that he can
contribute his changes.

I think that's very good news for this almost dead project. So please,
if anyone out there is also interested in these things, give it a try,
check it out, and report your results on this list. That'll help Edouard
to get his stuff tested and to find and fix bugs.

Thanks, Edouard, for contributing your work!
