> * What card have you used to develop the module ?

Terratec Cinergy 600

> * Have you some cards to suggest ?

No, any card with saa6588 should work.

> * Are the bttv (bt8x8) cards better than saa7134 ?

No, for video or TV the SAA7134 is better. bttv cards have the advantage that 
the RDS driver is included in the stable kernel.

> * Have saa7134 some sort of signal quality system ? Do you know some
> hardware with signal detection ?

Well, all these chips have some sort of signal strength detection. The result 
is available through the V4L2 API. That's what scantv uses, for example.

Technically, you can query the signal strength using the VIDIOC_G_TUNER ioctl. 
Here's the description from the API manual:
http://www.linuxtv.org/downloads/video4linux/API/V4L2_API/spec/r9122.htm
