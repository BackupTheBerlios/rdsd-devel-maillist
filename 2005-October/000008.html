Today, the new stable kernel 2.6.14 was released. It now includes the saa6588 
module required for RDS access. There is a new configuration option in the 
drivers/video4linux menu to compile this module.

At the moment, only bttv based cards can make use of the module. I hope to add 
saa6588 support to saa7134 as soon as possible.

During the last few weeks I was busy enhancing the RDS user space software. 
The whole chain kernel-rdsd-librds-rdsquery works now! I will probably commit 
the whole stuff at the beginning of next week. I will try to create a 0.0.1 
alpha release within November. I know, progress is slow, but my time for this 
is unfortunately very limited.

Have a good time,

Hans
