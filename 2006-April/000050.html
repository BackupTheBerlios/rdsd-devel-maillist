Am Dienstag, 25. April 2006 22:46 schrieben Sie:
> hello,
> i have a terratec cinergy 600 with the saa6588 rds decoder. your module
> inserts himself very well in the kernel (no error messages) 

This just means the module could be loaded and it found a saa6588 on the i2c 
bus. It doesn't mean that rdsd can actually read RDS data from /dev/radio. 
The card driver must be changed to use the saa6588 module. I have done that 
for bttv (is in the stable kernel now) and started work for saa7134, but 
haven't finished that yet. As the cinergy 600 is a saa7134 card - bad luck. 
You'll have to wait a little. I hope I can make some progress during the 
LinuxTag (May 3.-6.). 

> but data is 
> only available with the syslog with module option "debug=4".

If you see valid data there, then the saa6588 module works.

>
> how can i read rds data from the saa6588 and your driver ? qtradio
> doesn't see the rds function. i have no /dev/i2c-0 :( is there a program
> to do that ? i tried rdsd but only the gstat command (in telnet) shows
> something.

/dev/i2c support is kind of experimental until now. rdsd cannot work with a 
saa7134 card right now. If it outputs something other than an error message, 
then this is a bug :-)

I suggest you subscribe to the rdsd-devel mailing list and ask further 
questions there. I will keep you informed when saa7134 support is ready. It 
is on the very top of my priority list.
