> parameters are card=54, tuner=5).

Are you sure this card has an RDS decoder? The manufacturers website says 
nothing about it...

>
> To have a start, I started my radio app to tune in a station (used the
> German station EinsLive) and then I used the read_rds.c to get
> something to see. But the output is the following:
> Radio device opened, trying to read...
> Read returns -1
> Terminating.

Before you can read anything, you have to load the module:

modprobe saa6588

Have a look at dmesg to see if the driver actually found an SAA6588 RDS 
decoder.

If it says that an SAA6588 is found and it still doesn't work, please post 
again and we'll proceed.
